Health and Safety in Electricity

Health and safety in electrical work environments is a priority to protect individuals from risks associated with electricity. It is essential to follow safety measures to ensure a safe environment for everyone


Inspecting Devices: Regularly inspect and maintain electrical devices to prevent hazards.


Shock Prevention: Use protective gear like insulated gloves and boots.


Safety Training: Provide training on handling electrical hazards safely.


Accident Response: Ensure workers know how to respond to electrical fires or shocks.


Mental Health Support: Offer mental health support to address work-related stress.
